Smart City website for India

A Smart City is when municipality uses technology as internet of things (IoT), machine learning and automation to increase operational efficiency and improve the quality of government services and citizen welfare.

Iași Smart City is a informative website built in partnership with Iași City Hall that presents the progress made by the local authorities in implementing smart solutions for Iași. Both implemented and to do items are classified in 8 Smart City verticals: Governance, Energy, Buildings, Mobility, Infrastructure, Technology, Health Care and Citizen.

Award-winning project at Smart Citizen / Civic Engagement category within Smart City Industry Awards 4th edition which took place on December 11th, 2019.
